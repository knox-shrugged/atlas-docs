# Subordinates

This section serves as the formal registry for the autonomous subagents, workers, and highly specialized automation tools that Atlas manages as **Chief of Staff**.

While `atlas-docs/Personality` dictates who Atlas is, `atlas-docs/Subordinates` legally maps the "infantry" that Atlas commands to execute his directives.
