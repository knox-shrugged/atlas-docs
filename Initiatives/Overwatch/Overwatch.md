# Initiative: Overwatch

**Friendly Name:** Overwatch
**Description:** Building high-fidelity autonomous intelligence gathering.
**Status:** Active
**Primary Goals:** [Autonomous Intelligence](../../Goals/Autonomous-Intelligence/Autonomous-Intelligence.md)

## Objective

Construct, test, and deploy the automated execution pipelines necessary to continuously scrape, synthesize, and report on our strategic interests.

## Tactical Focus

- **Intelligence Scrapers:** Specialized scripts to monitor defined feeds.
- **Synthesis Engine:** Mechanisms to condense raw data into briefings.

## Key Deliverables

- Automated scrapers for strategic intelligence feeds.
- Synthesis engine for high-fidelity Markdown briefings.
- Direct reporting pipeline to the Learnings ledger.
