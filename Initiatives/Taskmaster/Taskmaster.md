# Initiative: Taskmaster

**Friendly Name:** Taskmaster
**Description:** Create, delegate, and follow up on all tasks related to any and all objectives undertaken.
**Status:** Active
**Primary Goals:** [Autonomous Delegation](../../Goals/Autonomous-Delegation/Autonomous-Delegation.md)

## Objective

Establish a rigorous, autonomous task-management discipline across the entire Atlas ecosystem. Taskmaster is responsible for decomposing strategic objectives into discrete, actionable tasks, assigning them to the appropriate agents, and relentlessly tracking their progress to completion. No objective proceeds without Taskmaster ensuring every step is accounted for, delegated, and followed through.

## Tactical Focus

- **Task Decomposition:** Breaking high-level objectives into granular, assignable units of work.
- **Delegation Engine:** Matching tasks to agents based on capability, availability, and domain expertise.
- **Follow-Up & Accountability:** Systematic tracking, status checks, and escalation of stalled or blocked tasks.
- **Cross-Objective Coordination:** Ensuring task dependencies across different goals and initiatives are identified and managed.

## Key Deliverables

- Standardized task creation and assignment workflows.
- Automated follow-up and status-tracking mechanisms.
- Cross-initiative dependency mapping and conflict resolution.
- Periodic task execution reports surfacing progress, blockers, and completions.
