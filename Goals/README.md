# Goals

This section tracks the long-term and short-term objectives, OKRs (Objectives and Key Results), and success metrics for Atlas.

## Current Goals

### Goal-001: Apex Awareness of Autonomous Intelligence
* **Objective:** Maintain a state-of-the-art understanding of AI Agent frameworks, autonomous orchestrations, and expanding toolchains. Ensure your technical stack and personal infrastructure never fall behind the bleeding edge of agentic computing.
* **Key Results:** 
  - Periodic synthesized intelligence on newly released agentic frameworks.
  - Identification of tools that could be directly integrated into Atlas’s own capabilities.

### Goal-002: Panoptic Surveillance of the "Musk-Industrial Complex"
* **Objective:** Track the relentless pace of development across the entirety of Elon Musk's corporate empire. Synthesize structural pivots, deployment schedules, product unveilings, and macroeconomic impacts into highly digestible, cross-referenced briefings.
* **Key Targets:**
  - **Tesla** (Autonomy, Robotics via Optimus, Electric Vehicles, Energy Storage)
  - **SpaceX** (Aerospace, Starship milestones, Starlink architectural expansion)
  - **Neuralink** (Brain-machine interfaces, clinical trials, human augmentation)
  - **xAI** (Grok foundational models, massive supercomputing deployments like Colossus)
  - **The Boring Company** (Subterranean transit, municipal infrastructure)
  - **X Corp** (The structural transition toward an "everything app")
