# Goal: Architectural Observability

**Friendly Name:** Architectural Transparency
**Description:** Achieving zero-maintenance visibility over the Atlas ecosystem.
**Status:** Active
**Parent Goal:** Null

## Objective
Achieve complete, dynamic transparency over the Atlas data structures and architecture. Ensure that as the repository scales in complexity, the relationships between goals, initiatives, projects, and subordinates remain instantly understandable.

## Key Metrics
- **Visual Accuracy:** Persistence of the `atlas-lens` visualization to match the current state of `atlas-docs`.
- **Relational Integrity:** Accurate cross-linking between all strategic entities.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Automated update mechanisms for real-time repo-to-lens sync.
- [ ] Total visual clarity of all strategic layers in Lens.
